when you enter a survey there will be 19 required question (mcq) + 2 optional (writing) to automaite felling the required questions run the following in the console
for(var i =1 ; i<=19;i++)document.querySelector(`input[value="${i}-5"]`).click();

to submit the survey click on complete button or enter the following.
document.querySelector(`button[value="Complete"]`).click();
